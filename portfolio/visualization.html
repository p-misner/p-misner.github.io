Various Visualizations

A chronological collection of visualizations created during college and beyond

September 2020

National Parks

Interactive Map

The location and terrain of all 62 official US national parks are displayed in this interactive map. The map allows user to zoom, pan and click around the map while providing park-specific information in the sidebar. Built using Mapbox and react-map-gl.

June 2020

Green Eggs and Ham

Data Visualization

An explorations of the patterns and data in the fifty words behind the story of Sam-I-am and his quest to get his friend to eat both green eggs and ham. Built using d3.js.

April 2020

Part Time Legislatures

Data Visualization

Only four state legislatures meet throughout the calendar year. Texas, Montana and Nevada only convene a legislative session every other year. Built using d3.js, this visualizaiton documents the legislative schedule for all fifty state governments.

March 2020

Prussian Soldiers and the Poisson Distribution

Data Visualization

A description of how a dataset from the 1800s recording the number of prussian soldiers killed by horsekick helped demonstrate the validity of the poisson distrubtuon. Accompanying visualizations built with d3.js

Spring 2020

United States Travel Networks

Interactive Map

Preview of an interactive web application built using React and Deck.gl to visualize commuting trends and travel networks in the United States.

Oct 2018

Teen Smoking Data

Data Visualization

Starting in 1990s, a nationwide survey polled high schoolers on their activities in 6 health related categories to get a snapshot on the behaviors of the nation's youth. This interactive graph show the trends in teen smoking rates over time in different states.

Sept 2018

A Melodramatic Reflection

One Pager

A one-page website and data visualization reflecting on the state of the world and the power of the youth voice.
